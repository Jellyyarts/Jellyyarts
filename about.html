About Me

Who I am

Introduction

My name is Jellyyarts or for short Jellyy (creative mix as a username I know).

I am currently in college trying to get an assocciates in graphic design to work for companies or do commissions for illustrations and logos alike.

A little background

I started working with art around 6 or 7 years ago in middle school, I've improved drastically since then though.

I have started to research more about anatomy and shading than when I first started and simply wanted to make things.

A reason why I'm still drawing is because I like to make scenarios or stories for characters that I like.

In terms of my art

How I draw

The best and easiest way I've found to draw is by making either small doodles in my sketchbook or spend full hours on my laptop working on a single drawing that will never see the sun again.

Usually I draw digitally on Photoshop and Ibis Paint X but occasionally make some other drawings or doodles in my sketchbook and have worked on a few projects for Adobe programs while doing certification courses for them.
